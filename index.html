About

Our family plantation. Your family table.

GoldenPalm is the flagship label of GM Akibor Limited — a family-run plantation milling fresh fruit bunches within hours of harvest. The result is a deep-red, nutrient-rich oil with the unmistakable aroma of authentic Nigerian cooking.

  • Cold-pressed, chemical-free extraction
  • Traceable from seed to shipment
  • RSPO & NAFDAC certified
